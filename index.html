Common Questions

Straight answers to
straight questions

If something isn't covered here, call or email us — we answer directly, without the runaround.

How is Primexa different from other IT providers?

We differentiate through a genuine security-first approach, modern automation tools, and 40+ years of combined team experience. We've delivered multiple Essential Eight rollouts and take a proactive rather than reactive approach — many providers only act when something breaks. We act before it does.

What is the Essential Eight and why does it matter?

The Essential Eight is the Australian Cyber Security Centre's baseline framework for protecting businesses from cyber threats. With the government increasingly tying grants and compliance requirements to security maturity, having a proper Essential Eight implementation protects your business and keeps you eligible. We've delivered multiple rollouts and guide you through the whole process.

Do you work with businesses that already have an internal IT person?

Absolutely — in fact, many of our clients do. We operate in a co-managed model where your internal staff handle day-to-day matters, and we provide specialist depth in security, cloud, compliance, and complex projects. It gives you a full technical bench without the full-time headcount cost.

What does your guaranteed P1 response SLA actually mean?

A human engineer responds to critical incidents around the clock — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Not an automated acknowledgement, not a ticket queue. Response time commitments are defined in your service agreement and are contractually binding with financial remedies if missed.

Are there lock-in contracts?

No. We offer month-to-month engagements because we believe in earning your business every month through consistent performance, not locking you in contractually. That said, many clients choose longer terms for pricing certainty — the choice is yours.

How quickly can you get us onboarded?

Our structured onboarding typically takes two weeks from engagement. In that time, we deploy monitoring tools, conduct a security and infrastructure audit, activate backups, verify your disaster recovery capability, and brief your team. We move fast without cutting corners.
